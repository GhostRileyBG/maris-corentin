House of Desolation : Lotus Society

Escape Game Mystery 3D Secrets Unity VR

Project introduction

Step into the mysterious world of House of Desolation, an immersive VR escape game where you play as an archaeologist unraveling the secrets of the enigmatic Lotus Society to find your lost son. Developed in just three weeks as part of a Unity course, this game challenges you with intricate puzzles and hidden mysteries.

My Role

I designed and implemented the gameplay mechanics, with a particular focus on developing the interactive puzzles of the second room. My role was also to optimize the immersion of the player while ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience.

Gameplay mechanics

Simon Says : Focus on memory and pattern recognition.
The player must reproduce a sequence of colors to validate the sequence. The game consists of 5 randomly generated sequences and restarts from the beginning if a sequence is failed.

Simon Says :
Le joueur doit reproduire une séquence de couleurs pour valider la séquence. La partie est constituer de 5 séquences générée aléatoirement et recommence de zéro en cas d'échec d'une séquence.

Tools and Technologies

Unity : Main game engine, used to integrate VR features and create gameplay interactions.
C# : For programming the mechanics of puzzles and interactions.
Oculus Quest 2 : VR headset used as the main platform for the game, providing an immersive virtual reality experience.
Jira : Project management tool, used to organize tasks and track progress.
Discord : Team communication to coordinate the technical and creative aspects of the project.
Github : Version control platform used to manage and collaborate on the project's codebase, allowing team members to track changes, fix issues, and ensure code stability throughout development.
