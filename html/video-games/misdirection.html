Misdirection : Nature's Last Hope

Walking simulator Sci-fi 3D Nature Unreal Engine 5 Narrative

Project introduction

The government of Alcaria announces the launch of a revolutionary technology that will change its citizens' lives forever. But as the city improves, doubts begin to arise about this perfection. Nonetheless, a young boy, isolated from these doubts, is about to make a surprising discovery when he encounters an unusual animal. This creature seems to be calling him to a mysterious place.
